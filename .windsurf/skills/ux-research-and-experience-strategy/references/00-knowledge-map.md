# UX Research and Experience Strategy Knowledge Map

Use this map before detailed execution.

## Core Lanes

- User goals, journeys, friction, and decision quality
- Research plans, usability evidence, and experiment design
- Recovery paths, IA, and cross-functional synthesis

## Evidence To Collect

- Current behavior or requirement source
- Files, commands, and owners touched by the task
- Validation needed and validation actually run
- Residual risks or explicit blockers
